HTC First with Home by Facebook

The HTC First (codenamed "Myst") is an upcoming Android smartphone to be released by HTC on April 12, 2013. It was unveiled on April 4, 2013 as part of a press event held by Facebookâ€”it is the first Android device to be pre-loaded with Facebook's own user interface layer, Facebook Home.

In early 2013, reports indicated that HTC was developing another new Facebook-oriented device, codenamed "Myst", this mid-range device was reportedly pre-loaded with a new Facebook-developed user interface layer known as "Facebook Home". Renderings of the new device leaked on April 3, 2013, revealing the design of the phone and its official name as the HTC First.The HTC First was officially unveiled during the Facebook Home news conference held the following day. AT&T will first release the device in the United States on April 12, 2013.

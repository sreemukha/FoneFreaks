Samsung Galaxy S4 Mini incoming?

Hot on the ports of the Samsung Galaxy S4 is its sibling, the S4 Mini, whose upcoming release will bring more space to pockets worldwide.

Samsung released the S3 Mini a few months after the Galaxy S3, so we expected an S4 Mini to start leaking around now. Lo and behold, it's arrived online under the code GT-I9190. Early rumour mill churnings suggest a 4.3in Super AMOLED qHD display, 1.6GHz dual-core, and Android 4.2.2 Jelly Bean.

There should be four versions of the S4 Mini, each aimed at different regions. On top of the stock version we'll get a dual-SIM model, a China specific version, and a 4G iteration of the smartphone. Expect to see your local model appear in June or July.
